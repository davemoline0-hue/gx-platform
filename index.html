About Us

Dave Gallioth

Founder & Developer

Hello! I'm Dave Gallioth, born on October 20, 2007. I'm a young technology enthusiast who loves creating innovative solutions that bring people together.

GX Social Network was born from my passion for technology and my desire to create a platform where people can connect, share experiences, and build meaningful relationships. As someone who grew up in the digital age, I understand the importance of having safe, engaging spaces for social interaction.

What inspires me most is seeing how technology can transform the way we communicate and build communities. Every feature in GX is designed with this vision in mind - to create a platform that's not just another social network, but a place where genuine connections happen.

My journey in technology started at a young age, and through GX, I hope to inspire other young creators to pursue their passions and turn their ideas into reality.
